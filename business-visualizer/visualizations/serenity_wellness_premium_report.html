The Psychology Behind Our Approach

Every design decision is backed by behavioral science.

Commitment & Consistency
Once someone books a class, they're 80% more likely to show up. The app creates a public commitment.
Source: Robert Cialdini, Influence
Variable Rewards
Streak counters and badges trigger dopamine. Users become addicted to maintaining their streak.
Source: Nir Eyal, Hooked
Social Comparison
Leaderboards and shared achievements tap into competitive instincts - 'If they can do it, so can I'
Source: Leon Festinger, Social Comparison Theory
